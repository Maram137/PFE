About Eventoo!

Eventoo is an event digitalization platform, suitable for both in-
person and hybrid events. Designed for corporate event
organizers, it ensures audience engagement, exhibitor/sponsor
satisfaction, and optimization of the digital experience through
various features and smart tools.
