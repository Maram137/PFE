About BIGTECH!

In view of the record attendance and the diversity of the
communities it has invited to its 7th edition, the TDS
organizing committee has decided to take its
involvement in technology to the next level by joining
forces with other summits in this case AFRRIC'UP, the
African Startup Summit, DEVELOPER DAYS, the African
Developers and Engineers Expo-Conference, GEEC, the
gaming Expo and e-sport championship, and JOB FAIR,
the skills and career development hub, to create the
African technology and business hub BIGTECH, the
biggest event organized by Tunisia, dedicated to
technology and startups on the African continent.
